# Academic Integrity Checklist

- Do not invent data, citations, methods, approvals, limitations, or results.
- Mark unsupported claims as `MATERIAL GAP`.
- Mark user-dependent facts as `NEEDS CONFIRMATION`.
- Distinguish `USER DATA`, `SOURCE CLAIM`, and `INFERENCE`.
- Keep limitations visible.
- Use exact citation support levels: direct, partial, or background.
- Do not describe generated figures as experimental evidence.
