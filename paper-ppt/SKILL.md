# Paper PPT

## 输入

- manuscript or paper notes
- audience
- duration
- required format

## 输出

- slide outline
- speaker notes
- figure placement plan
- unresolved asset gaps

## 规则

- 不改变论文结论。
- 不把未验证 claim 做成核心 slide。
